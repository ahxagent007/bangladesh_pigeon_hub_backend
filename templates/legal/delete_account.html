Account & Data Deletion

BD Pigeon Hub  ·  AlphaCue Technologies  ·  Last updated: June 6, 2026

You have the right to delete your BD Pigeon Hub account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains exactly what happens when you request deletion and how to do it.

How to Delete Your Account

There are two ways to request account deletion:

Option 1 — From the App (Recommended)

1

Open the app and go to your Dashboard

Tap the Me tab in the bottom navigation bar, then tap Profile Settings.

2

Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom of the Profile Settings screen. Tap Delete Account (shown in red).

3

Confirm your password and confirm deletion

Enter your password to verify your identity, then tap Permanently Delete. This action cannot be undone.

4

Done — you will be signed out immediately

Your account is queued for deletion. All personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Option 2 — By Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email from the email address registered with your account:

To: alphacuetechnologies@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request — [your username]

Include in the body:
  • Your registered username
  • Your registered email address
  • Confirmation that you want your account and all data permanently deleted

We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

What Gets Deleted

The following table shows exactly what data is deleted and what (if anything) is retained:

Data Type Action Notes
Account profile (username, email, password, phone, bio, avatar) Deleted Within 30 days
Your pigeon profiles and photos Deleted Images removed from server storage
Your active listings Deleted Listings marked inactive, then removed
Your wall posts and images Deleted All post images removed from server
Your comments and likes Deleted
Your notifications Deleted
Your pedigree records Deleted Linked pigeons' sire/dam fields set to null
Your private messages Deleted Full conversation history removed for all participants
Your bids on auctions Deleted If auction is still live, it is cancelled first
Offer and payment TXN records you were party to Retained 1 year Required for financial record-keeping and dispute resolution. Retained in anonymised form.
Server access logs containing your IP address Retained 90 days Standard security log retention. Deleted after 90 days.
Google Analytics data Retained by Google Anonymised aggregate data — cannot be individually identified or deleted by us.

Timeline

  • Immediately: Your account is deactivated. You are signed out on all devices. Your public profile and listings are no longer visible.
  • Within 30 days: All personal data listed as "Deleted" in the table above is permanently removed from our databases and servers.
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirming completion of deletion.

Before You Delete

Consider the following before deleting your account:

  • Active listings: Any active pigeon listings will be removed. Interested buyers will no longer be able to contact you through the platform.
  • Active auctions: If you have an ongoing live auction, deletion will cancel it. Bidders will be notified.
  • Won auctions: If you have won an auction and not yet completed the transaction, resolve this before deleting your account.
  • Accepted offers: Complete any accepted offers and payment confirmations before requesting deletion.
  • No recovery: Deleted accounts cannot be recovered. Your username will be permanently released.

Contact
